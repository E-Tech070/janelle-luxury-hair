Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

We want you to love your purchase. Because hair products are a personal-care item, our return policy is a little different from general merchandise — here's exactly how it works.

Hygiene Items — Wigs, Bundles, Closures & Frontals

For hygiene reasons, wigs, bundles, closures, and frontals cannot be returned or exchanged once the packaging has been opened or the item has been worn, installed, or altered in any way (cut, dyed, or styled). This is standard practice across the hair industry and helps us maintain quality and safety for all our customers.

Accessories

Unused accessories (clips, headbands, scrunchies, etc.) in their original condition and packaging can be returned within 3 days of delivery.

Wrong or Damaged Items

If you receive the wrong item, a damaged item, or something different from what you ordered, we will make it right — this is the one case where a hygiene item can still be returned or exchanged. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with:

  • Your order number
  • A clear photo or video of the item received
  • A brief description of the issue

Once confirmed, we'll arrange a replacement or refund at no extra cost to you.

How Refunds Work

Approved refunds are sent back to the bank account or transfer method you paid with, and are typically processed within 3–5 business days once approved. We'll keep you updated via WhatsApp or email throughout.

How to Start a Return or Report an Issue

Message us on WhatsApp or use the live chat on your account page — please include your order number so we can look into it quickly.
