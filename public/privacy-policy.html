Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Janelle Luxury Hairs & Accessories ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you use our website, why we collect it, and how it's handled.

Information We Collect

  • Account details you provide: name, email address, phone number, delivery address
  • Order information: items purchased, order value, payment proof screenshots you upload
  • Messages you send us through the on-site chat or AI assistant
  • Reviews and photos you choose to submit for products you've purchased

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To confirm payments and send order status updates by email
  • To respond to your messages and support requests
  • To display your reviews (with your name) to other shoppers, once approved

How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored securely in our database and is never sold to third parties. Passwords are encrypted and are never stored or visible in plain text, even to us. Payment proof screenshots are only visible to our admin team for the purpose of confirming your order.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate this site, including hosting (Render), database storage (MongoDB Atlas), transactional email delivery (Resend), and our AI shopping assistant (Anthropic). These providers only receive the minimum information needed to perform their function.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us using the details below. You can also update your account details directly from your account page while logged in.

Cookies

We use minimal local browser storage to keep you logged in and to remember items in your shopping cart. We do not use tracking cookies to sell your data to advertisers.
